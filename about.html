About Us

Patil's Restaurant is a family-rooted restaurant in Sangli, founded by Mrs. Ashwini patil.

Inspired by western culture, we blend western recipes with innovative twists, creating a menu that caters to diverse palates. Our commitment to quality is reflected in using locally sourced, fresh ingredients.

From crispy burgers to fusion snacks, each item on our menu tells a story of flavor, tradition, and passion. Beyond the delicious offerings, our restaurant embodies hospitality, community, and a love for good food. Join us at Patil's Restaurant for a culinary journey.
